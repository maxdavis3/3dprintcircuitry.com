CAPABILITIES

Electronics Printing Services

01

3D Printed PCBs

Aerosol jet and inkjet-printed conductive traces on flat and 3D substrates. Silver nanoparticle ink sintered to 4–6 µΩ·cm resistivity. Multi-layer boards with blind and buried vias — no drilling, no etching.

02

Embedded Component Printing

Resistors, capacitors, and inductors printed directly into the circuit substrate. Eliminates discrete component placement for space-constrained IoT and wearable designs. Component values tunable post-print via laser trimming.

03

Conformal & 3D Electronics

Circuits printed on curved, flexible, and 3D-formed substrates. Antennas on product housing surfaces, strain sensors conforming to structural geometry, and biosensing electrodes on wearable forms.

CONDUCTIVE MATERIALS

Printable Electronics Materials

Electrical performance depends on the ink. Our material portfolio covers signal, power, and sensing applications across the spectrum of substrate types.

  • ● Silver nanoparticle ink — 4 µΩ·cm resistivity, high conductivity
  • ● Copper nanoparticle ink — cost-effective, solderable
  • ● Carbon ink — piezoresistive sensing and EMI shielding
  • ● PEDOT:PSS — transparent, flexible electrode applications
  • ● Dielectric inks — interlayer isolation and encapsulation
  • ● Stretchable conductor for epidermal electronics
